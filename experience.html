ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

International Student Conference (ISC)

The International Student Conference (ISC) is one of the world's longest-running international student organizations, established in 1954 and held annually in Tokyo, Japan. It brings together students from universities worldwide to discuss and develop policy proposals on pressing global issues.

ISC provides a platform where students engage in structured debate, cross-cultural dialogue, and collaborative problem-solving — submitting their proposals to international institutions. Evan served in two progressive roles over two years, growing from Public Relations to Chief of General Affairs.
